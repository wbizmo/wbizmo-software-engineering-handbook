# Fun Facts

1. Google popularized Site Reliability Engineering.
2. Many outages are detected by users before engineers.
3. Observability extends beyond monitoring.
4. Distributed tracing became critical as microservices grew.
5. Alert fatigue is a major operational challenge.
6. MTTR is often more important than preventing every failure.
7. Large companies run dedicated incident command systems.
8. Reliability engineering is now a specialized discipline.
