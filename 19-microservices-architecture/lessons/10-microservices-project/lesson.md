# Lesson 10: Microservices Project

## Project

Design a production-grade microservices platform inspired by your portfolio projects.

Choose one:

- SyncGrid
- VaultBox
- inFlowForge
- NOMMO

or combine concepts from multiple projects.

---

## Objectives

Design:

- service boundaries
- APIs
- communication flows
- event architecture
- observability strategy
- resilience strategy
- deployment strategy

---

## Required Services

Create at least:

1. Identity Service
2. Core Business Service
3. Notification Service
4. Analytics Service

---

## Architecture Diagram

Draw:

Clients

↓

Gateway

↓

Services

↓

Databases

Include communication paths.

---

## Service Boundaries

Define:

- ownership
- responsibilities
- APIs
- databases

---

## Communication Design

Choose:

- REST
- gRPC
- Events
- Queues

Explain why.

## Data Strategy

Define:

- database per service
- event synchronization
- reporting strategy

---

## Resilience Strategy

Include:

- rate limiting

## Observability Strategy

Include:

- logs
- metrics

- alerts

## Deployment Strategy

- scaling

- rollback process
