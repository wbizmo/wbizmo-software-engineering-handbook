# Fastify & Express APIs Fun Facts

1. Express is one of the most popular Node.js web frameworks.
2. Fastify is known for performance and schema-based validation.
3. Many modern products are API-first.
4. REST APIs commonly use nouns for resources.
5. API security is one of the most important backend skills.
6. Health checks are used by hosting platforms and load balancers.
7. Many SaaS companies expose APIs as part of their product.
8. Your SyncGrid, VaultBox, and inFlowForge projects are examples of API-heavy backend systems.
