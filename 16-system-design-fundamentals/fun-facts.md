# System Design Fundamentals Fun Facts

1. Most large outages are caused by system interactions rather than code bugs.
2. Netflix operates thousands of microservices.
3. Google processes billions of searches daily.
4. CAP Theorem is one of the most discussed concepts in distributed systems.
5. Many successful systems begin as monoliths.
6. Caching can reduce database traffic by more than 90%.
7. Event-driven architectures are common in modern cloud platforms.
8. Reliability engineering has become a dedicated discipline at large companies.
