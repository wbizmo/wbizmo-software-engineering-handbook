# Fun Facts

1. Netflix operates thousands of microservices.
2. Amazon popularized service-oriented architectures internally.
3. Many successful startups begin with monoliths.
4. Most microservice failures come from organizational and operational issues, not coding mistakes.
5. Observability becomes dramatically more important as service counts grow.
6. Conway's Law influences architecture more than many engineers realize.
7. Distributed monoliths are one of the most common architecture mistakes.
8. Microservices are an organizational strategy as much as a technical strategy.
